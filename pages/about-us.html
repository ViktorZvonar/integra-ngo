What We Do

INTEGRA was created as a response to the needs of y people, especially those in difficult situations. Its aim is to strengthen the life and social competences of them so that they can find their way on the life long education. The organization aims to carry out supporting activities in the field of employment, education and youth.

We create various local programs, projects and events using a non-formal approach in the fields of education, culture, entrepreneurship, sports and healthy living. We are actively involved in Lebork's green movement. Our primary focus is on individuals from diverse backgrounds and age groups. Together with them, we design workshops and initiatives covering a wide range of topics, such as education, human rights, active citizenship, participation, democracy, tolerance, preventing radicalization, promoting diversity, multiculturalism, sports and fostering a healthy lifestyle.

Our organization collaborates with local educational institutions, as well as local municipalities, local organizations involved in youth and adult education and culture, European institutions and educational sectors. Regularly, our organization organizes meetings and workshops with local partners to plan social activities, including petitions, environmental protection initiatives, awareness campaigns, flash mobs and more. We conduct workshops on project design, focusing on disseminating project results, presenting programs, brainstorming ideas, and drafting project plans.

INTEGRA has dedicated members who are very motivated and well-educated. They also have experience in both national and international projects, along with strong managerial abilities for handling project management and execution.

The team of INTEGRA possesses extensive experience in various EU Programs, with a particular focus on youth-related initiatives. Our staff members have demonstrated proficiency in leading and executing projects across diverse thematic areas. What distinguishes our team is the sense of unity and collaboration that proves our work environment. With a clear administrative structure, decision-making processes our organization is transparent and inclusive, ensuring that all relevant matters are addressed effectively.
